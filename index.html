Which city

Durban

Bustling Durban is the hub of the Kwa-Zulu Natal’s business and industry and pulses with all the energy of a major port city. Luxury hotels abound on Durban’s beachfront, and this city is often referred to as South Africa’s Miami Beach. Durban is one of the fastest-growing urban areas in the world. Its port is the busiest in South Africa and also one of the 10 largest in the world. Durban is blessed with balmy weather all year round, making it a perfect holiday paradise. The beachfront is bordered by holiday accommodation and 5 Star hotels and luxury apartments, all of which have an idyllic view of the Indian Ocean.

Historical places at Newcastle

Majuba College

Majuba TVET College offers a wide variety of courses as well as leanership programmes. It consists of 8 campuses in the township and at town Students are always safe, secured, support services such as on-course academic support and sports are provided.

The Carnegie Gallery

The Gallery is a public Art Museum situated in the Central Business District of Newcastle, Kwa-Zulu Natal. It has paintings, sculptures, ceramics, fibre art, beadwork and wooden carvings by RSA artists. A collection of African Art & Zulu material cultural objects has also been accumulated.

Ngagane river

Ngagane River is next to Duck Ponds and is located in Amajuba District Municipality, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The iNgagane River is also used to supplement the raw water supply to the Ngagane WTP. It is used as the Stormwater Run-off Zone.
